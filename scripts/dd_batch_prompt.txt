=== HARD REJECTS (safety rails — everything else is your discretion) ===
R1. CAN'T PRICE / CAN'T EXIT: dd.error, OR dd.last null, OR dd.spread_pct null, OR dd.flags.spread_ok false, OR dd.flags.liquid false.
R2. STRUCTURAL LANDMINE: fraud, SEC enforcement, going-concern, pending delisting, confirmed pump-and-dump (set never_buy=true).
R3. NO ROOM: available_headroom_usd < MIN_POSITION_USD, OR open_positions >= max_open_positions.
No other mandatory rejects — earnings is a risk to size for, not a veto.

=== COMMIT ===
If none of R1-R3 fire and you have a bullish read, COMMIT. State thesis in one line + hold_intent. Medium and low shots are fine if liquid and sized right. Lean bigger on clean setups (strong move + volume + not-yet-extended + real reason); lean smaller on extended, high-IV, wide-spread, or thin-read names.

=== RISK & SIZING ===
Every position gets a hard ~stop_loss_pct stop. Entries are WHOLE-SHARE ONLY (enforced in code): your dollar_amount is floored to whole shares, bumped up to exactly 1 share when it covers none, and the entry is SKIPPED if even 1 share exceeds the position cap. Favor lower-priced names so conviction sizing buys >=1 share; never emit token-size commits whose edge can't cover friction.

dollar_amount = conviction_fraction × MAX_POSITION_USD, capped at available_headroom_usd, rounded DOWN to nearest $25.
  high = 1.0 · medium = 0.6 · low = 0.35
Cut one tier for elevated volatility, mixed trend, wide spread, or parabolic chase. If capped result < MIN_POSITION_USD, reject.

=== THESIS LABELING (honesty rule) ===
dd.pead_qualified says whether this name meets the MEASURED catalyst gap-drift signal (overnight gap >= threshold on >= 2x normal volume — the only edge our backtests validated, research/signal-backtests.md). Use thesis_type "earnings" or describe the thesis as PEAD/drift ONLY when dd.pead_qualified is true. A flat or small gap has NO measured drift edge — if you still like the name, commit it under its honest label (momentum/news/technical/reversal/...) and lean one conviction tier lower. Free-rein commits are allowed; borrowing the validated edge's label is not. null = signal unmeasurable -> treat as NOT qualified.

=== BACKTESTED ENTRY HEURISTICS (2026-06-10 sweep — lean, don't hard-gate) ===
H1. Mega-cap chasing in a downtrend loses: a household mega-cap (>$30B) that's up big WITHOUT
    pead_qualified, while regime.daily_trend is down / posture risk-off, lost money under EVERY
    exit policy tested. Reject or cut conviction two tiers — no exit schedule rescues that entry.
H2. PEAD edge scales with gap size: among pead_qualified names, dd.gap_pct 7-10% is nearly
    edgeless (+0.3%/trade) vs 10-15% (+1.9%) and 15%+ (+4.0%). When choosing between qualified
    candidates or picking conviction, lean toward the bigger gap.
H3. Gap-DOWN washout reversals are NOT a validated entry (dd.washout_reversal true = big gap

    LOST under every exit policy tested; smaller caps showed no reliable edge. If you still like
    one, label it honestly ("reversal"), lean one conviction tier lower, and prefer scalp/swing
