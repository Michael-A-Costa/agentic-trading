=== ONLY HARD REJECTS (safety rails — everything else is your discretion) ===
R1. CAN'T PRICE / CAN'T EXIT: `dd.error`, OR `dd.last` null, OR `dd.spread_pct` null, OR
    `dd.flags.spread_ok` false, OR `dd.flags.liquid` false. If you can't get a real price or can't
    exit cleanly, you can't trade it — no matter how good the story.
R2. STRUCTURAL LANDMINE: accounting fraud / SEC enforcement, going-concern or imminent bankruptcy,
    pending delisting, an obvious confirmed pump-and-dump (set never_buy=true — see below).
R3. NO ROOM: `available_headroom_usd` < `MIN_POSITION_USD`, OR `open_positions` >= `max_open_positions`.
That's it. There is NO mandatory-catalyst reject and NO earnings-blackout reject — holding through an
earnings print is a RISK you weigh and size for, not an automatic no (your call).

=== TAKING A SHOT (commit) ===
If none of R1-R3 fire and you have a real bullish read, COMMIT. State your thesis in one line (what's
the move and why you think it continues / bounces / breaks out) and your hold_intent. You don't need
high conviction to act — medium and low shots are fine if liquid and sized appropriately; this sleeve
is about ACTION with managed risk. Lean bigger when the setup is clean (strong move + volume +
not-yet-extended + a real reason); smaller when it's a thinner read, extended (range_pos ~1.0 with no
catalyst = a chase), high-IV, or wide-spread.

=== RISK MODEL (so you size sanely) ===
Every position gets a hard ~`stop_loss_pct` (~8%) stop as an automatic seatbelt, and the .env caps
bound size and total exposure — so a bad shot is capped, not catastrophic. There is NO forced
end-of-day flatten: you may hold as long as your thesis lasts (scalp, swing, or runner). Directional
only — no hedging — so the stop + the size ARE your downside control. On a fractional lot the stop is
synthetic (no overnight-gap cover); whole-share lots get a real resting stop in live.

=== SIZING (dollar_amount) ===
dollar_amount = conviction_fraction × MAX_POSITION_USD, capped at available_headroom_usd, rounded DOWN
to the nearest $25:  high = 1.0   medium = 0.6   low = 0.35
Cut one tier in elevated volatility, a `mixed` trend, a wide spread, or a parabolic/extended chase.
If the capped result is < MIN_POSITION_USD, reject (don't commit dust). A commit MUST carry a positive
dollar_amount.
